# Shopify 独立站售中客服话术

> 专业的 Shopify 独立站售中客服话术模板，涵盖订单确认、支付处理、发货通知、物流追踪、订单修改等售中服务场景。

## 使用说明

- 话术中的 `[变量]` 需要根据实际情况替换
- 独立站需要主动发送订单状态更新，不要等客户来问
- 信息要完整，减少客户二次咨询

---

## Shopify 售中话术列表（共 10 条）

### 1. 订单确认

**适用场景：** 客户下单后确认订单信息

```
Hello! Thank you for your order from [Brand Name]!

Order #[Order Number] has been confirmed.

Order Details:
• Items: [Product list]
• Total: [Amount]
• Payment: [Payment method] - Confirmed
• Shipping to: [Address]

What's Next:
We'll process your order within 24-48 hours and send you a shipping confirmation with tracking number. You can view your order status anytime at: [Order status link]

Questions? Reply to this message or email us at [Email]. We're here to help!
```

```
• Order: #[Order Number]
```

### 5. 物流追踪查询

```
Hello! I'd be happy to help you track your order #[Order Number].

Current Status: [Status - e.g., In Transit]
Location: [Current location]
Last Update: [Date/Time]

How to Track:
1. Click this link: [Tracking link]
2. Or visit [carrier website] and enter: [Tracking Number]
3. Or log into your account at [Store URL]/account

Tracking updates every 12-24 hours. If you don't see updates for more than 3 days, please let me know and I'll investigate with the carrier.
```

### 10. 国际订单清关说明

**适用场景：** 国际订单清关状态说明

```
Hello! Here's an update on your international order #[Order Number].

Current Status: In Customs Clearance
Location: [Country/City]

What This Means:
Your package is going through standard customs inspection. This is normal for international shipments and typically takes 2-5 business days.

What You May Need to Do:
• Customs may contact you for additional documentation
• Import duties/taxes may apply (varies by country)
• Keep your phone/email accessible for customs notifications

Estimated Delivery: [Date range] (after customs clearance)

Need Help?
If customs requests any documents, let me know and I'll provide whatever you need. We're monitoring your shipment and will update you on any changes.

Thank you for your patience with international shipping!
```

## 使用技巧

1. **主动沟通**：不要等客户来问，主动发送订单状态更新
2. **信息完整**：订单确认要包含所有关键信息
3. **提供链接**：给出可点击的追踪链接，而不只是追踪号
4. **管理预期**：说明追踪更新频率和可能的延误情况
5. **品牌化表达**：在话术中融入品牌元素，体现独立站特色
