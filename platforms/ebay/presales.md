# eBay 售前客服话术

> 专业的 eBay 售前客服话术模板，涵盖 Top Rated Seller 说明、Best Offer 处理、产品咨询、买家保护政策等售前场景。

## 使用说明

- 话术中的 `[变量]` 需要根据实际情况替换
- eBay 对响应速度要求严格，1 小时内回复能保持 Top Rated Seller 资格
- 使用正式英语，避免缩写和俚语

---

## eBay 售前话术列表（共 10 条）

### 1. Top Rated Seller 说明

**适用场景：** 买家首次咨询或对卖家信誉有疑虑时

```
Hello! We're a Top Rated Seller on eBay with 99.8% positive feedback. This means we've consistently provided excellent service, fast shipping, and accurate item descriptions. You can buy with confidence knowing you're dealing with a trusted seller. Thank you for your interest!
```

---

### 2. 快速发货承诺

**适用场景：** 买家询问发货时间

```
Hello! We offer fast shipping - your order will be shipped within 24 hours of payment confirmation. We use reliable shipping carriers to ensure your item arrives safely and on time. Estimated delivery time is [timeframe]. Thank you for your purchase!
```

---

### 3. Best Offer 处理

**适用场景：** 买家提交 Best Offer 报价时

```
Hello! Thank you for your Best Offer of $[amount]. While we appreciate your interest, our best price is $[counter-offer]. This price reflects the quality and value of the item. Would you like to accept this counter-offer? We're here to help with any questions!
```

---

### 4. 退货政策说明

**适用场景：** 买家询问退货政策

```
Hello! We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund. Returns are easy - just contact us and we'll provide a return label. Your satisfaction is our priority!
```

## 使用技巧

1. **快速响应**：1 小时内回复询盘，保持 Top Rated Seller 资格
2. **专业礼貌**：使用正式英语，避免缩写和俚语
3. **强调保护**：主动提及 eBay Money Back Guarantee
4. **数据说话**：用具体数据（如 99.8% 好评率）建立信任
5. **避免承诺**：不要做出平台政策外的承诺
